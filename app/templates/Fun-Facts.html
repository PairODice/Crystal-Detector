Quartz

  • SiO2
  • Hexagonal prism with a pyramidal termination
  • Used in abrasives as well as to transmit UV light in optics
  • Transparent, orange, smoky brown, purple most common

Garnet

  • A nesosilicate group with the general formula of X3Y2(SiO4)3
  • Most commonly dodecahedral but also trapezohedral or hexoctahedral
  • Gemstones and abrasives
  • Variety of colors: mostly reddish shades, orange, and pink as well

Fun Fact

Though it may seem as a single mineral, garnet is actually a group of similar minerals

  • Used commonly in crystal radios and in batteries
  • Gold, shiny
