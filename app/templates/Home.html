What is Crystal Vision?

Crystal Vision is an innovative AI powered app to aid amateur and professional Geoscientists by streamlining rock and crystal classification and recognition. It works by recognizing commonalities between myriad specimens of a mineral, such as corundum, and works to find an efficient way of identifying it, such as crystal shape and growing patterns.
